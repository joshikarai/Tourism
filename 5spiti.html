🍃Key Monastery🍃

Key Monastery is a historic Buddhist monastery located in Spiti, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance.
The best time to visit is during the summer months when the weather is pleasant and the monastery is accessible.

🍃Chandratal Lake🍃

Chandratal Lake is a high-altitude lake located in the Spiti district of Himachal Pradesh, known for its crystal-clear waters and surrounding mountain scenery.
The best time to visit is during the summer months when the lake is accessible and the weather is pleasant.

🍃Hikkim Village🍃

Hikkim Village is a remote village located in the Spiti district of Himachal Pradesh, known for its unique culture and pristine natural beauty.
The best time to visit is during the summer months when the weather is pleasant and the village is accessible.
