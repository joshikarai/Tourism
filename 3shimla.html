🍃The Ridge🍃

The Ridge is a popular tourist destination in Shimla, known for its scenic beauty and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
The best time to visit is during the summer months when the weather is pleasant and the skies are clear.

🍃Mall Road🍃

Mall Road is the main shopping street in Shimla, lined with numerous shops, restaurants, and cafes.
Thebest time to visit is during the summer months when the weather is pleasant and the streets are bustling with activity.

🍃Kufri🍃

Kufri is a hill station located in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, known for its natural beauty and adventure sports.
The best time to visit is during the winter months when the snow-capped mountains and ski slopes are at their best.
